    Recipe

    Step 1: In a bowl we mix the ingredients of the homemade hamburgers. The eggs will give them a touch of extra creaminess while the breadcrumbs, which, as you can see, is a small amount to maintain this creaminess, will give a touch of crunch. Garlic and onion, pocho previously, so that it does not taste too wild although, if you prefer, you can add it raw. We mix with clean hands


    Step 2:Now we divide the dough into 4 equal portions. We knead them one by one, quickly passing it from one hand to another (thus, we get the meat protein to develop, preventing the hamburger from falling apart during cooking) and then, we crush them a little, giving it the shape of a hamburger
    Step 3:We cook them. In a nonstick skillet, we put just a drop of oil, over medium heat and place the hamburgers on it. The exact cooking time will depend on the desired point of the meat. If we want it undercooked, with 3 minutes for each of its faces will be enough. If we want it more done, double the time. When we turn it around, we put a slice of cheese on top of the hamburger, so that it melts.
